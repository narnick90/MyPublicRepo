Job Description: Senior ASP.NET Developer with Strong SQL Expertise

Position Overview:
We are seeking an experienced and highly skilled Senior ASP.NET Developer with a proven track record of at least 8 years in designing, developing, and maintaining complex web applications using ASP.NET and an excellent command of SQL. As a key member of our development team, you will play a critical role in the design, implementation, and optimization of our web-based software solutions.

Responsibilities:

    Software Development:
        Design, develop, and maintain scalable and high-performance web applications using ASP.NET, C#, and related technologies.
        Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including UI/UX designers and front-end developers, to deliver robust and user-friendly applications.
        Participate in the full software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering and analysis to deployment and post-release maintenance.

    Database Management:
        Develop complex SQL queries, stored procedures, and functions to optimize data retrieval and manipulation processes.
        Ensure efficient data storage, integrity, and security within the application by utilizing best practices for database design and optimization.
        Identify and resolve performance bottlenecks in database operations and optimize query execution.

    Technical Leadership:
        Provide technical leadership and mentorship to junior developers, guiding them on best practices, coding standards, and design patterns.
        Collaborate with architects and technical leads to define technical strategies and make informed decisions about technology stack and architecture.

    Code Quality and Testing:
        Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code that adheres to industry standards and coding guidelines.
        Conduct thorough testing, including unit testing and integration testing, to ensure the reliability and stability of applications.

    Troubleshooting and Debugging:
        Investigate and resolve complex technical issues related to ASP.NET applications and SQL databases.
        Perform root cause analysis of production issues and implement appropriate fixes and enhancements.

    Continuous Improvement:
        Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends, technologies, and best practices, and apply them to improve development processes and product quality.
        Identify opportunities for process improvement, automation, and innovation within the development lifecycle.

Qualifications:

    Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field. Master's degree is a plus.
    Minimum 8 years of professional experience in ASP.NET development, C#, and SQL.
    Strong expertise in designing and optimizing SQL Server databases, including complex queries, performance tuning, and indexing strategies.
    Proven experience in developing web applications using ASP.NET MVC or ASP.NET Core.
    Familiarity with front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and front-end frameworks.
    Solid understanding of software design principles, patterns, and best practices.
    Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex technical issues.
    Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
    Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and Agile/Scrum methodologies.
    Relevant certifications (e.g., Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate) are a plus.

Benefits:

    Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
    Comprehensive benefits package, including health, dental, and retirement plans.
    Professional development opportunities and support for continued learning.
    Dynamic and inclusive work environment fostering innovation and creativity.
    Opportunity to work on challenging projects that have a real impact on the organization.

If you are a seasoned ASP.NET developer with exceptional SQL skills and a passion for creating high-quality software solutions, we encourage you to apply and become a key contributor to our dynamic development team.
